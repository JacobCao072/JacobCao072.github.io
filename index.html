Jacob Cao

Biology

With a deep commitment to providing exceptional dental care, Dr. Jacob Cao brings over 15 years of experience in general dentistry to his many patients. Known for his skilled manual dexterity and patient-focused approach, Dr. Cao prioritizes patient well-being and oral health, empowering his patients to maintain healthy smiles.

Dr. Cao completed his Doctorate in Dental Surgery (DDS) at the esteemed University of the Pacific, where he graduated at the top of his class. His belief that every smile is unique drives him to tailor each treatment plan to meet his patients' individual needs and goals, ensuring exceptional, personalized results.

In his practice, Dr. Cao merges advanced dental technology with a welcoming and relaxed environment, offering a comprehensive range of services from routine cleanings and fillings to cosmetic treatments such as veneers, teeth whitening, and Invisalign. He ensures that patients with dental anxiety can experience treatments in complete comfort.
